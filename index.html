  • Damping and Natural Frequency of Beams (Engineering) - Damping is the dissipation of energy of a material or system under cyclic loading, thought of as the restraining of vibratory motion. This project will be an experimental device for future engineering students to observe how mass locations change the damping of the beam and has been nominated in the LACCEI conference.
